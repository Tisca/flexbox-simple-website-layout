Hello, my name is Tisca.

I am a trained graphic designer and aim to work as a web designer. That's why I take online courses and practice daily. Currently, I am learning how to code responsive websites.

Being creative is part of who I am. As a kid, I loved to draw, write tales and make up entire worlds. The Neverending Story (the film when I was about five years old, the book when I was twelve) was formative for me. And, of course, all the cartoons and animations that have come out since the late '80s.

I was fascinated by the characters and tried to imitate special effects with colored pencils and brushes. During my training, I came into contact with graphic design software for the first time. The world of vector graphics was love at first sight - and a relief, because until then, I had tried to draw perfect outlines by hand.

Since then, I have gained experience designing for print, fashion, and screen. When creating corporate designs for companies, sometimes a simple website was part of the whole package. That was my introduction to the magic of coding. At one point, I worked closely with the development team and was even able to help design a minigame!
